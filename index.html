Predatory Birds

Bald Eagle

The largest and most American bird. Can smell freedom from over 10 miles away.

Golden Eagle

Pretty chill guy. Known amongst the natives as "Big Brown Bird."

Hawk

Likes to kill squirrels and rabbits and stuff. Banned from Walmart.

Falcon

Can dive at speeds up to 240 mph, which I guess is pretty fast.

Horned Owl

This owl is common in the United States and is pretty easy to catch apparently.

Snow Owl

I think this is the owl that they used in the Harry Potter movies.

Osprey

Can fly around with its wings and peck things with its beak.

Harrier

Looks similar to the golden eagle. Not to be confused with the fighter jet.

Goshawk

The Goshawk is pretty much just a big falcon with crazy, orange eyes. Thirsts for blood.

Vulture

Eats dead animals, but not before circling over them for a bit.

Rooster

I ran out of cool birds, so I had to use this rooster. The Alice in Chains song is good though.

Velociraptor

Possible ancestor of other predatory birds. Slightly more dangerous than the Goshawk.
